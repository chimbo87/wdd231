Site Plan: EcoTravel Hub

Site Name

EcoTravel Hub

This name was selected because it directly communicates our focus on sustainable travel while suggesting a comprehensive resource center. The word "Hub" implies a central gathering place for eco-conscious travelers, making it memorable and purpose-driven.

Site Purpose

EcoTravel Hub serves as a comprehensive platform that will:

1. Provide detailed information about sustainable travel options and eco-friendly accommodations

2. Offer interactive tools for calculating and offsetting travel carbon footprints

3. Share educational resources about responsible tourism practices

4. Connect eco-conscious travelers with verified sustainable tour operators and accommodations

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"As a environmentally conscious traveler planning a vacation, I want to find eco-certified accommodations in my destination. Where can I find verified sustainable lodging options?"

Scenario 2:

"As a travel planner, I need to calculate the environmental impact of different transportation options for my client's trip. How can I compare the carbon footprint of flying versus taking a train?"
